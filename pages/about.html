About

We aspire to become a global payment network through building the portfolio of payment institution licenses in every country and uniting them under one digital umbrella. We are focused on the introduction of new improved banking services to the underbanked business community of small and medium-sized enterprises. We see our future in establishing long-term relationships with our customers based on trust, transparency, and reciprocity.

Cepheus

Strategy

We intend to provide fast an efficient service for small and medium-sized businesses, as well as for personal accounts through Fintech innovations. Our focus is on solving problems, rather than selling financial products.

We at Cepheus Global Payments believe that creating a company where people love to work is built on relationships, growth, and impact. We cultivate a culture of low ego and a high bar for excellence. We’re committed to making sure diversity and inclusion is always at the heart of our company’s mindset.
